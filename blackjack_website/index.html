Blackjack Simulator

Rules:

The goal is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over it. You win if you have a hand higher than the dealer's while being below 21, you win. If you exceed 21, you "bust", meaning you automatically lose.

There are three moves:

  • Hit: Recieve a new card
  • Stand: Keep your current hand
  • Split: Split two identical cards into two separate hands

Card values:

  • J, Q, and K: All 10s
  • 2 to 10: Face value
  • A: 1 or 11*

* Note: totals of hands containing aces will be calculated as its highest possible value without going over 21.

Progression:

  1. You place a bet that abides by the minimum and maximum limits.
  2. The dealer hands a card face-up to you player and a card face-down for themselves.
  3. The dealer hands another card to you and themselves, with both cards face-up this time.
  4. You decide if you want to STAND or HIT. You may hit as many times as you want (as long as you don't go over 21)
  5. The dealer flips his first card. If their total is 16 or less, they must hit. Otherwise, they stand.
  6. Determine payouts depending on cards.

Payout rules:

  • If your hand is better, you get double your bet back.
  • If your hand is worse, you lose the money you betted.
  • If your hand and the dealer's have the same value (including 21), the dealer PUSHES, meaning you just get your bet back.
  • If you get a blackjack and the dealer doesn't, you get paid out 3:2 (If you bet 2 chips, you get 3 on top of that, totalling to 5 chips)
